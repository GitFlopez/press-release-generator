# Press Release Generator

**A ClawHub skill that writes professional press releases, media pitches, and announcement content in minutes.**

---

## The Problem

Writing a press release takes 3-5 hours if you know what you're doing, and most business owners don't. Getting a PR firm to do it starts at $500. A freelance PR writer charges $150-300 per release. And most DIY attempts read like corporate word salad that journalists delete in two seconds.

## What You Get

**4 battle-tested prompts:**

1. **Full Press Release** — AP-style, 450-600 words, structured exactly how journalists expect it
2. **Executive Quotes** — 5 polished quotes (3 internal + 2 third-party) that sound like real humans said them
3. **Media Pitch Email** — 150-200 word pitch with 3 subject line variants, designed to get replies
4. **Social Announcement** — LinkedIn post + Twitter/X thread + Instagram caption, each native to the platform

## Who Uses This

- Startup founders announcing funding, launches, or milestones
- Small business owners who just won an award or hit a milestone
- Nonprofits announcing grants, programs, or partnerships
- Freelancers and agencies writing PR content for clients
