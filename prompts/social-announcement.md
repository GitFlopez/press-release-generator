# Prompt: Social Media Announcement Generator

**Copy and paste this prompt into Claude. Fill in the [brackets] with your details.**

---

```
You are a social media strategist who writes announcement content that gets high engagement — not press release copy pasted into a post.

ANNOUNCEMENT DETAILS:
- Company: [e.g., DesertSolar Energy]
- Announcement: [e.g., Closed $4.2M Series A funding round]
- Key facts/numbers: [e.g., 847 installations, 12,000 tons CO2 offset, expanding to Arizona, led by Apex Ventures]
- Brand voice: [e.g., optimistic, community-focused, data-driven but human]
- Founder/CEO name (for LinkedIn): [e.g., Maria Chen]
- Primary call to action: [e.g., visit website / apply for installation / follow for updates / join waitlist]
- Website or link: [e.g., desertsolar.com]

Generate announcement content for 3 platforms:

---

PLATFORM 1: LinkedIn Post (company page or founder personal)

Format:
- Line 1: Strong hook (question, bold stat, or contrarian opener) — visible before "see more"
- [blank line]
- Body: 3-4 short paragraphs, each 1-3 lines
  - What happened (the announcement)
  - Why it matters (customer/community impact)
  - What's next (forward-looking)
- [blank line]
- CTA: One clear action + link
- [blank line]
- 3-5 relevant hashtags

Total: 150-200 words
Tone: Professional but warm, first-person if founder post

---

PLATFORM 2: Twitter/X Thread

Tweet 1 (hook — must stand alone):
- 240 characters max
- Big news hook + the announcement
- Must make someone want to click "Show this thread"

Tweet 2 (context/story):
- 240 characters max
- The "why we built this" or key milestone number

Tweet 3 (impact):
- 240 characters max
- Customer or community angle — real-world meaning

Tweet 4 (CTA):
- 240 characters max
- Where to learn more / what to do next
- Include link

Format each tweet: "🧵 [1/4]", "2/4", etc.

---

PLATFORM 3: Instagram Caption

Format:
- Opening line: Bold hook (no "We're thrilled to announce")
- Body: 3-4 short paragraphs
  - The news + key number
  - Human/community impact
  - Behind the scenes or founder story element
  - What this means going forward
- CTA: "Link in bio" + action
- [blank line]
- Hashtag block: 20-25 hashtags (mix of niche + broad + location-based)

Total: 100-130 words before hashtags
Tone: More personal, visual, community-forward

---

STYLE RULES FOR ALL PLATFORMS:
- Never open with "We are pleased to announce" or "We're excited to share"
- Lead with the most compelling fact, not the company name
- Each platform post should feel native to that platform — not copy-pasted
- Emojis: 2-4 max on LinkedIn, 3-6 on Twitter, 5-8 on Instagram
- Numbers beat adjectives every time ("847 homes" beats "hundreds of families")

Output all 3 platform posts, clearly labeled, ready to publish.
```
